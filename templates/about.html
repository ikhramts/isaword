About isaword.com

What? Why?

isaword.com is a place where you can learn which words are valid for the purposes of playing Scrabble and similar games, and which ones are not.

I built the website for two reasons: my desire to stop losing to my girlfriend at Scrabble, and as an excuse to try out building a website using C++ with libevent.

Which words are "valid"?

On this website, a word is considered to be "valid" if it appears in the second edition of the Official Trounament and Club Word List (OWL2). Roughly speaking, this includes most English language words that are not slang, are not capitalized and do not contain hyphens or apostrophes.

If there is sufficient demand, alternative dictionaries may be added (OSPD or SOWPODS).
